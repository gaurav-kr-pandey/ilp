It was this realization that prompted me to address this very real need of the student community and the Industry itself. We at ILP strongly believe that with the right guidance and perfect determination, any student willing to learn gets its dream job. In the last few years, we have helped several students to achieve their goal and made them the darling of the industry. At ILP, all our instructors are themselves great coders and highly employable in the Industry. They decided to join ILP because they are passionate about teaching and strongly believe in our vision. Whether you are a student just out of school, a college/university student, a working professional planning to switch domain to Software/ICT, or just someone wanting to enhance ICT skills, I welcome you to join ILP.
